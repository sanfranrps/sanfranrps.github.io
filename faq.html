Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the San Francisco Roleplay server?

You can join by connecting through our Discord server at discord.gg/Vk4wkgnad3.

Are there age restrictions to play?

We require all members to be at least 13 years old in accordance with Discord guidelines and server rules.

How do I apply for Staff?

Head over to the Recruitment tab on our website or click the "Apply for Staff" link to fill out our official application form.
